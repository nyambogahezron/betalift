# Betalift Issue Guidelines

Thank you for taking the time to help improve Betalift! Please follow these guidelines when creating or managing issues.

## Reporting a Bug

- **Search first:** Check if your issue has already been reported.
- **Title:** Use a clear, descriptive title.
- **Description:** Include steps to reproduce, expected vs. actual behavior, and any relevant logs or screenshots.
- **Environment:** Specify your OS, device, and app/server version if applicable.

## Requesting a Feature

- **Title:** Use a concise, descriptive title.
- **Description:** Explain the feature, its purpose, and how it would benefit users.
- **Alternatives:** List any alternative solutions or workarounds you've considered.

## Issue Triage

- Issues are reviewed by maintainers and may be labeled (e.g., `bug`, `enhancement`, `question`).
- Please respond to requests for more information.
- Issues may be closed if they are duplicates, invalid, or inactive.

## Best Practices

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Provide as much detail as possible.
- Use [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for contribution guidelines.

Thank you for helping us make Betalift better!
